• To register or add classes: Enter the course reference number (CRN) in the Add Class Worksheet below. Click Submit Changes to register.
  • To find a class: Click Class Search.
  • To drop a class: Click on the pull-down menu arrow next to the course. Click on Submit Changes to drop the class. If the option to drop is not available, then the class may not be dropped via the Web.
  • Registration Errors: If there is a problem with your registration, you will see a message indicating a time conflict or the need for instructor's signature.
  • Prerequisites: Check the course schedule for prerequisites. It is your responsibility to be sure you have met all the prerequisites for your courses.
  • Registration Cancellation: Please make arrangements with the Business Office if your student account has a balance due. Otherwise, your registration may be canceled.

    You must be enrolled for at least 12 billing hours for Fall and Spring Semesters to be eligible for many Federal and State financial aid programs. If you have any questions contact the Financial Aid office.
